The Culture and Community in Santa Barbara

The Culture and Community in Santa Barbara When I think of Santa Barbara, I think of family. Santa Barbara is where all my family resides. This town is comprised of large families, most who have lived there for many generations. The Mexican culture is displayed throughout the city, primarily through its people and historic architecture.
Section 3
Santa Barbara Cuisine

Santa Barbara is known for its authentic, Mexican cuisine. The town is filled with tons of Mexican restaurants, street taco trucks, family-owned taquerias, you get the picture! The food is phenomenal, and Mexican food is my personal favorite!
Section 4
Places to Visit

Santa Barbara is recognized as one of the most beautiful towns in the United States. From the beaches to the mountains, people come from all over the world to spend a weekend here. One of the most recognized landmarks is the Santa Barbara Mission. I feel incredibly lucky to have grown up here.
