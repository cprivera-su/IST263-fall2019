Places to See and Explore!

Image Below: The Santa Barbara Hotel


Are you looking for a peaceful break from reality? Visit Santa Barbara! The city of Santa Barbara attracts millions of tourists throughout the year! I have even seen cruise ships dock in my town! Around the town are community skate parks, historic buildings (for example, The Old Mission Santa Barbara), beach volleyball courts and open track and field that are accessible to the public, and so much more! In Santa Barbara, there is truly so much you can do with your family!

Image Below: Butterfly Beach, located in Montecito, CA.


If you ever do decide to visit my town, you have to pay a visit to Butterfly Beach. This beach is one of the most beautiful yet peaceful beaches in all of California. Across the street lies the Four Seasons Hotel in Montecito, CA. Many, many more celebrities have visited this beach, along with millions of tourists from around the globe. A few celebrities that have been spotted at Butterfly Beach include the Kardashians, Kevin Hart, David Beckham, Ashton Kutcher, and so many more! Whenever I need a break, need to clear my thoughts, or want a beautiful view, I come to Butterfly Beach. Now, there are also many many beaches around Santa Barbara. All of the beaches in town are beautiful and clean; however, this is a favorite among most!

Come visit beautiful, sunny, Santa Barbara!
