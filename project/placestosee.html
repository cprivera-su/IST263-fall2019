Quick Background

Santa Barbara, CA is recognized as one of the most beautiful cities in the United States. This small beachy town is filled with rich Spanish history and beauty all around.

Explore

There are many local beaches surrounding the city of SB. The beach is a common hot spot where you will find locals and tourists from near and far! Personally, my favorite beach is Butterfly Beach, located near the Montecito community.

Visit!

If you ever find yourself in Santa Barbara, you must visit the historic Old Mission! The Santa Barbarba Mission is a spanish Mission, founded by the Franciscan order on December 4, 1786. This beuatiful building serves as apiece of histroy in the town. The SB mission is known as the "Queen of California Missions", with its double grand bell towers, beautiful gardens, and mountain backgrop.

Places to Dine and Shop

Ever in the mood to wander around and explore? Then you have to visit State Street. State Street is the hub of SB, filled with places to shop, eat, relax, listen to music, see a show, you get the picture! There is amazing Mexican cuisine in the town. You will also see lots of seafood restaurants, steakhouses, and pop-up cafes!
