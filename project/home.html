Image Below: Downtown Santa Barbara

Explore my website to discover great places to dine, relax and unwind. The focal point of Santa Barbara is the downtown area, otherwise known as State Street. The town's post popular avenue is covered with unique shopping, delicious food and gorgeous architecture. There are plenty of activities to do with your loved ones on State Street. Whether you are exploring art galleries, theaters, or other local attractions like The Stearns Wharf, you’re sure to find something you’ll love here!

Image Below: The Santa Barbara Sunken Gardens

No trip to the American Riviera is complete without visiting the Sunken Gardens. The gardens is located at the Santa Barbara Courthouse, which is also recognized as one of the country’s most beautiful buildings. When visiting the Gardens, you might as well look around the courthouse. Built in 1929, this building has been labeled as one of the “grandest Spanish Colonial Revival structure ever built!"
