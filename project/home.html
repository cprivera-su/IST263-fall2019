About Me

Personal Profile:

Hello, my name is Celine Rivera. Up until college, I have lived my entire life on the West Coast. I was born and raised in Central California, in a town called Santa Barbara. However, I decided to venture off and experience a different life for my college years. For the past three years I have been adjusting myself to the East Coast lifestyle and have been loving my new normal. But, that doesn't mean I don't get homesick from time to time. I have created this site to give you, the reader, a look at my life at home. Enjoy!
