Where to Eat

The food in SB is like no other! Here, you'll find authentic Mexican food, delicious seafood resturants, and mouth-watering steakhouses.

Favorite Spots to Dine

Looking for a delcious and affordable taco stand? La Super Rica is possibly the most notorious place to eat at in town! At any time, you will always see a line out the door. Want a smoothie on the go? Blenders in the Grass is the perfect spot for you. Grab a large, fresh fruit smoothie of your choice for only $5! In the mood for some fresh seafood? We recommend you trying the Fish House This place has great service, beautiful views, and delicous seafood dishes that are always fresh!
